Business Context

This analysis examines employee performance data across an Education company operating in multiple regions. The HR department has requested insights into departmental distributions to support strategic workforce planning decisions.

Key Objectives:

  • Calculate frequency count for the HR department
  • Visualize department distribution across the organization
  • Support resource allocation and recruitment strategies
  • Provide data-driven insights for executive decision-making

Dataset Overview

The dataset contains 100 employees with the following attributes:

  • employee_id: Unique identifier (EMP001-EMP100)
  • department: Sales, Operations, Marketing, HR, IT, Finance
  • region: Middle East, Latin America, Africa, Asia, Europe, North America
  • performance_score: Employee performance rating (0-100)
  • years_experience: Years of work experience
  • satisfaction_rating: Employee satisfaction (1-5)

# Create histogram showing distribution of departments
